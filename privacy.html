Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

This website provides anonymous one-on-one chat services. We are committed to protecting user privacy.

Data We Process

We do not require registration or personal identification. Users may enter a temporary nickname which is not stored permanently.

However, for technical and security purposes, the system may automatically process limited data such as:

  • IP address
  • Device / browser information
  • Connection timestamps

Use of Data

This data is used only for:

  • Establishing and maintaining chat connections
  • Preventing abuse, spam, and misuse
  • Ensuring security and proper functioning of the service

Data Storage

We do not store chat messages after a session ends. Conversations are temporary and deleted automatically upon disconnection.

We do not sell, share, or transfer user data to third parties.

User Rights

Users have the right to request information about their data or request deletion where applicable.

Cookies & Local Storage

This site uses browser localStorage only to remember your age-verification confirmation so you are not asked again on every visit. No tracking cookies are used.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Consent
